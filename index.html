The Super Guessing Game!

This project was designed as part of a Code Fellows assignment. When loaded in a browser, the user will be asked a series of pre-determined questions. First, the user is asked a series of yes/no questions, then scored off their result. Then for the second part, the user is asked to guess different numbers.

Part I

Do I live in Seattle?

Do I like to ski?

Do I enjoy playing Eldritch Horror?

Am I Irish?

Part II

I'm thinking of a number between 1 and 10. Can you guess what it is?

How old am I?
